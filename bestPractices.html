Best Practices

Food Choices

Reduce Fast Foods. Vegan or vegetarian options are better for the climate. Minimize waste of food.

Air Travel

Air Travel is one of the highest polluters and generators of greenhouse gases. Consider alternate transportation options and make smart travel plans.

Commuting

Try commuting through public transport when possible. Rideshare or carpool. Consider biking or walking.

Showers and Baths

Take quicker showers as opposed to baths. Don't leave tap running.

Household Appliances

Unplug appliances and chargers when not in use.

Don’t wash clothes or dishes if load is not full.

Reduce and Reuse

Recycle and buy recycled products. Reduce purchase of disposable water bottles.

Pass along or share products that can have another life.
